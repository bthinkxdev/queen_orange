Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Queen Orange, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help. Please read our refund and return policy carefully.

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Time Frame: Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • Condition: Products must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Packaging: Items must be in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Hygiene Products: Undergarments and innerwear cannot be returned for hygiene reasons

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Innerwear, undergarments, and swimwear
  • Products marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Products with removed or damaged tags
  • Custom or personalized items

3. How to Initiate a Return

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email us at returns@queenorange.com or WhatsApp us at +91 98765 43210
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, product details, and reason for return
  3. Get Authorization: Wait for our return authorization and instructions
  4. Ship the Product: Pack the item securely and ship it to the address provided

4. Return Shipping

  • Defective/Wrong Items: We will provide a prepaid return label and cover all shipping costs
  • Change of Mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Lost/Damaged Returns: We recommend using a trackable shipping service

5. Refund Processing

Inspection and Approval

Once we receive your returned item:

  • We will inspect the product within 2-3 business days
  • You will be notified via email about the approval or rejection of your refund
  • If approved, your refund will be processed

Refund Timeline by Payment Method

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Refunds will be processed via bank transfer within 7-10 business days. Please provide your bank account details.
  • Online Payment (Razorpay/UPI/Card): Refunds will be credited to the original payment method within 7-10 business days
  • Wallet/Gift Cards: Refunds will be credited to your Queen Orange wallet within 2-3 business days

Refund Amount

  • Full refund for defective, damaged, or wrong items (including shipping)
  • Product cost refunded for change of mind (shipping charges non-refundable)
  • COD charges (if any) will not be refunded for customer-initiated returns

6. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Size Exchange: If you ordered the wrong size
  • Color Exchange: Subject to availability
  • Defective Products: We will send a replacement immediately

Note: Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the desired size/color is unavailable, we will process a full refund.

7. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Report Immediately: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide Evidence: Send clear photos/videos of the defect
  • Quick Resolution: We will arrange a replacement or full refund (including shipping) immediately

8. Wrong Product Delivered

If you receive the wrong product:

  • Contact us immediately with order details and photos
  • We will arrange pickup at no cost to you
  • Correct product will be shipped immediately with free shipping
  • Full refund option available if you prefer

9. Cancellation Policy

Before Shipment

  • Orders can be cancelled anytime before shipment
  • Full refund will be processed within 3-5 business days
  • To cancel, contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp

After Shipment

  • Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled
  • You can refuse delivery and initiate a return
  • Refund will be processed as per return policy

10. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Products with obvious signs of use
  • Products returned more than 7 days after delivery
  • Products with missing accessories or parts
  • Products returned without original packaging

11. Gift Returns

If the item was marked as a gift and shipped directly to you:

  • You'll receive a store credit for the value of your return
  • Original purchaser will be notified of the return
  • Credit can be used for future purchases

12. International Orders

Currently, we ship within India only. For any queries about international shipping in the future, please contact us.

13. Questions and Support

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: returns@queenorange.com or support@queenorange.com
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210
  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210
  • Address: 123, Fashion Street, M.G. Road, Bangalore - 560001

14. Payment Gateway Information (Razorpay)

For online payments made through Razorpay:

  • All refunds are processed through Razorpay's secure payment gateway
  • Refund amount will be credited to your original payment method (card/UPI/wallet)
  • Processing time: 7-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • You will receive a refund confirmation email from both Queen Orange and Razorpay
  • For any payment-related queries, you can also contact Razorpay support

15. Store Credit Option

Instead of a refund, you can opt for store credit:

  • Receive 100% value as store credit
  • Credit processed within 24-48 hours
  • Valid for 12 months from issue date
  • Can be used for any future purchase
  • No expiry extensions

16. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Note: This refund policy complies with Indian consumer protection laws and Razorpay's payment gateway requirements. Your satisfaction is our priority!
